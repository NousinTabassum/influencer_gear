Some Bangladeshi Influencers

Prito Reza

Prito Reza has worked as a photojournalist for some notable Bangladeshi dailies such as The Daily Ittefaq, New Age, The Daily Star, Drik Picture Library, Daily Ajker Kagoj and Bhorer Kagoj.Reza was born in 1984 in Bangladesh. He completed his SSC from Noakhali Zilla School and earned his graduation in photography from Pathshala South Asian Media Institute of Photography.

Sadman Sadik

Sadman Sadik in a Bangladeshi Youtuber, Online educator and internet personality. He is famous for his own YouTube channel Sadman Sadik, which has more than 150k subscribers and over 5 million views. He is is also Chief Content Creator of 10 Minute School.Sadman started his career as a Youtuber in 2017. He is a regular writer for “10 Minute School”, the largest online educational platform of Bangladesh.

Sakib Al Hasan

Shakib Al Hasan is a Bangladeshi international cricketer.Shakib started playing cricket at an early age. According to Prothom Alo sports editor Utpal Shuvro, Shakib "was fairly proficient at cricket and was often hired to play for different villages and teams".[10][11] In one of those matches, Shakib impressed an umpire who arranged for him to practice with the Islampur Para Club, a team in the Magura Cricket League. During the practice session, Shakib batted aggressively and bowled fast, as he usually did.
